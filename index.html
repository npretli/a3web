The Kawartha Lakes Hiking Project

The Kawartha Lakes region in Ontario has over 600 km of trails, many located within local conservation areas and the Ontario Parks system. This website is intended to provide information about a selection of these hiking trails and other outdoor activities available in the parks.

About the Project

This website was created by four GIS students from Fleming College who are passionate about hiking, the outdoors, programming, and mapping. It is also intended as a showcase of our developing web design and communication skills.
