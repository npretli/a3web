Hiking

The QEII Wildlands Provincial Park is a unmaintained park, which means it is free to explore! The Ganaraska Trail, however, does pass through the park and there are several access points.



The Ganaraska Trail Marked by white blazes, this trail traverses 500 kilometers of Canadian Wildernerss. There are many local clubs which lead guided hikes for many sections of trail. Aboout 100 kilometers of trail pass through QEII Wildlands PP and can be accessed from 3 locations: Victoria Falls, Devil Lake and Near Black Creek.
